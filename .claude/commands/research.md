# Research Command

Documents research findings and explorations.

## Format

Create files in `research/` with structure:

```org
#+TITLE: RES-XXX-Topic
#+DATE: [Date]

* Question/Topic

[What we're investigating]

* Background

[Context and prior art]

* Findings

[What we discovered]

* Conclusions

[Implications for the project]

* References

[Links and citations]
```

- Formal verification techniques
